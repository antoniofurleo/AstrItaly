Video Description:
In this 3-minute educational video, high school students learn how mathematics plays a crucial role in addressing Sustainable Development Goal 13: Climate Action. By applying concepts like algebra and data analysis, students explore how NASA Earth observation data can help track rising CO2 levels, temperature increases, and deforestation. Through real-world climate models, students discover how math can be used to predict future climate trends and develop solutions to reduce carbon emissions and fight climate change.
